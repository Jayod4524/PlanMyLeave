About PlanMyLeave

PlanMyLeave is an online leave management system designed specifically for Sri Lankan workplaces. It replaces traditional paper-based systems with a simple, user-friendly digital solution. Our goal is to streamline leave processes and make HR work easier and more transparent.

Built with modern web technologies, PlanMyLeave supports local leave types, multi-language interfaces, public holiday calendars, and real-time leave tracking. It encourages digital transformation in organizations and is scalable for future growth.
